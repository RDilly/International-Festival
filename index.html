12 noon - 4pm

Holy Name Parish in East Nashville announces our second annual International Festival and invites you to celebrate with us.

We will have ethnic food from countries all across the globe including: Poland, Egypt, Haiti, Ireland, Italy, Jamaica, Liberia, Mexico, Nigeria, Peru, Rwanda, Sudan, Canada, and the United States. There will be indigenous music, beer/wine, games and a silent auction.

Proceeds to Benefit:
Loaves & Fishes Food Program for the Homeless
Education Programs for Children in Africa
St. Antoine Parish in Haiti
